Photo Essay: Creation of Thanksgiving Dinner

Thanksgiving is more than a meal- it's a true labor of love. This photo essay showcases Gigi, a former Special Education teacher, making a wonderful Thanksgiving dinner in West Linn, Oregon. All photographs on this webpage were taken for a digital photography course.

Photos by Paul Stultz

Date Shot: November 28, 2024

© 2024 Paul Stultz. All Rights Reserved.

The Beginnings of a Pumpkin Pie

Gigi starts off Thanksgiving Day setting out ingredients for a classic pumpkin pie, adding the pumpkin puree not a minute later. "Making pies is one of my favorite parts of Thanksgiving," she shared as I began taking my first round of pictures.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/4.5, 1/60 sec, 30.0 mm

Adding Condensed Milk to the Mix

The Thanksgiving pie-making momentum continues, with Gigi carefully pouring sweetened condensed milk into her mix to complete the base for her classic pumpkin pie.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/5.0, 1/60 sec, 39.0 mm

The Pie of Pride

As the batter is finished, a pie is born- as well as the start of a sugary mess. The day is still getting started: can the kitchen keep up?

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/5.0, 1/60 sec, 41.0 mm

Peeling Sweet Potatoes: The Beauty within

Exposing the true nature of a sweet potato, the beauty- both inside and outside- is revealed, ready for Gigi's next dish.

Exposure Information: ISO 320, f/5.6, 1/60 sec, 55.0 mm

The Kitchen: Wide Shot

A step back tells the story of a well-kept, if not lightly cluttering kitchen. With a few more dishes to go, Gigi continues on.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/4.0, 1/60 sec, 18.0 mm

Candying the Sweet Potatoes

Maple syrup descends onto the soon-to-be tasty treat, to help the dish simmer properly.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/5.0, 1/60 sec, 34.0 mm

Ready for the Oven

Several sprinkles of brown sugar, and a few pats of butter later, the candied sweet potatoes are ready to simmer in the oven.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/5.6, 1/60 sec, 55.0 mm

An Accumulating Mess

Kitchen mess and clutter begin to take its hold- a good smelling one though. The recently melted butter, with a dash of fresh rosemary, contributes to a heavenly scent.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/4.0, 1/60 sec, 23.0 mm

Pan-demonium strikes

The culprit is caught in the act- a buttery pan! With cleanup set aside, the focus remains on completing the feast in time. Can Gigi have everything ready before family arrives?

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/5.0, 1/60 sec, 40.0 mm

Bagel Scouts the Kitchen

Up and about, Bagel the French Bulldog is on the move- making sure no trouble is afoot nearby the kitchen. Taking a brief stop, she silently lets the photographer know the perimeter is secure.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/5.6, 1/60 sec, 55.0 mm

A Pinch of Rosemary

The rosemary returns! Gigi demonstrates that sometimes, less is more in a dish.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/5.6, 1/60 sec, 55.0 mm

The Casserole Chronicles Begin

Gigi swiftly adds the main ingredients together for her green bean casserole, a longtime family favorite. "It wouldn't be Thanksgiving without the green bean casserole," Gigi shared during our interview.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/5.0, 1/60 sec, 40.0 mm

A Morning Array

Natural light casually filters in through the window as Gigi is ready to pop the last major dishes into the oven.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/5.6, 1/60 sec, 18.0 mm

A Bagel at Rest

Satisfied with her 'food safety' patrol, Bagel lays down by the Christmas tree to prepare for an afternoon nap.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/5.6, 1/60 sec, 55.0 mm

A Berry Elegant Display

Gigi adds the final touch- canned cranberry sauce presented unashamedly, in a crystal lotus dish.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/4.0, 1/60 sec, 23.0 mm

A Thankful Farewell

After hours of work, Gigi takes a moment to relax with a couple sips of champagne.

Exposure Information: ISO 400, f/4.0, 1/60 sec, 18.0 mm
