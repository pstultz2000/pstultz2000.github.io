The Feature: White Oak Savanna Trailhead

A Jeep Wrangler, parked alongside the White Oak Savanna trailhead in West Linn, Oregon, stands ready for autumn adventure among fall-time trees. The Jeep shows its true purpose in this frame: the outdoors. Whether the journey is the destination, or how to get there, the Wrangler takes a breather as a hiker continues their trek. Taken on November 7, 2024, for a digital photography course, this photo tells the story of feature photography- an empowering everyday connection to nature.

Photo by Paul Stultz

Date Shot: November 7, 2024

Exposure Information: ISO 200, f/7.1, 1/125 sec, 18.0 mm

Environmental Portrait: A Car of Pride

Paul, a student at Colorado State University, stands beside his 2010 Chevrolet Camaro 2SS, photographed on October 27, 2024, in West Linn, Oregon. A portrait of pride and showmanship, the car itself takes a stand in its element- the owner’s garage, as Paul honors the machine with a Portland Cars & Coffee shirt. Captured for a digital photography course, this image serves as a creatively correct demonstration of an environmental portrait.

Time Exposure: A Warm Welcome

Paul, a student at Colorado State University, uses a lighter to write out 'hi' and a phone light to create swirling strokes. This light painting captures dynamic movement and the contrasts of light in a dark bedroom. Taken on September 29, 2024, for a digital photography course, this photo demonstrates the use of long exposure to depict the passage of time through light trails.

Manual Exposure Exercise

Underexposed (-2 EV)
ISO 800, f/8, 1/1000 sec

Correct Exposure
ISO 800, f/8, 1/250 sec

Overexposed (+2 EV)
ISO 800, f/8, 1/60 sec

Using a rose bush as a colorful subject, I took these photos to demonstrate three different exposures: underexposed (-2 EV), correct exposure, and overexposed (+2 EV). Each photo was taken with the same ISO and aperture settings, with only the shutter speed adjusted to vary the exposure.

Taken in my backyard in West Linn, Oregon, for a digital photography course.
